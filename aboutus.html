A brief note about us and what we do.
 we are here to collect the donations for the covid patients welfare, without any benifits to us.

As apart of social service we are doing these things,as this pandemic (covid-19) affected many lives in our country and
many have lost their dear ones in this pandemic situation.

“The world is going through a period of crisis, but whether we look at it as a crisis or as an opportunity to reshape our thinking, depends on us.
So use this period as a lesson on how to live life with a concern for all of humankind.”

These donations will be utlized for the education of the childrens who lost their parents and also used for the food and medicines of covid patients.

These are the facilities we provide with donations

Education

The small childrens who lost their parents, will be provided education at free of cost,
We provide them a quality education.

Food

Food is the basic requirement, we provide the food to covid patients,
we provide healthy and hygenic food.

Medical

We provide medicines and oxygen cyliders to the needy people,
we also provide the vaccination facility to the poor people.
